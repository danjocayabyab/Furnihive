1. INTRODUCTION TO FURNIHIVE
================================================================================

Furnihive is an online marketplace for furniture. Whether you're looking to
furnish your home or sell your furniture products, Furnihive connects buyers
with sellers in one easy-to-use platform.

WHO CAN USE FURNIHIVE?

- BUYERS: Browse and purchase furniture from various sellers
- SELLERS: List and sell your furniture products
- ADMINS: Manage the platform and support users

================================================================================
2. GETTING STARTED
================================================================================

SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS

- Computer, tablet, or smartphone
- Internet connection
- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- JavaScript enabled

CREATING AN ACCOUNT

1. Go to the Sign Up page
2. Enter your email address
3. Create a password
4. Fill in your full name
5. Select your role: Buyer or Seller
6. Submit the form
7. Check your email for verification (if required)

LOGGING IN

Buyers and Sellers:
1. Go to the Login page
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click Sign In

Admins:
1. Go to the Admin Login page
2. Enter your admin credentials
3. Click Sign In

The system will automatically take you to your dashboard based on your role.

LOGGING OUT

Click the Logout button in the navigation menu or go to the Logout page.

3. FOR BUYERS - SHOPPING GUIDE
================================================================================

3.1 BROWSING FURNITURE

HOME PAGE
The home page shows featured products, promotions, and categories to explore.

SHOP PAGE
Browse all available furniture:
- Use filters to narrow by category, price, material, or style
- Search for specific items using the search bar
- Sort results by price, popularity, or newest items
- Click on any product to see details

PRODUCT DETAILS
When viewing a product, you'll see:
- Product images (click to zoom)
- Name, price, and description
- Specifications (dimensions, materials, etc.)
- Seller information and ratings
- Customer reviews
- Related product suggestions

3.2 SHOPPING CART

ADDING ITEMS TO CART
1. On the product page, select your desired quantity
2. Click "Add to Cart"
3. Continue shopping or go to cart

MANAGING YOUR CART
- View all items with images and prices
- Change quantities using plus/minus buttons
- Remove items by clicking the delete button
- See your subtotal, estimated shipping, and total
- Click "Proceed to Checkout" when ready

3.3 CHECKOUT

STEP 1: SHIPPING INFORMATION
- Enter your delivery address
- Select shipping method if options available

STEP 2: PAYMENT
- Choose your payment method
- Enter payment details securely

STEP 3: REVIEW AND CONFIRM
- Review your order summary
- Check all details are correct
- Click "Place Order" to complete purchase

After ordering, you'll see a confirmation page with your order number.

3.4 YOUR ACCOUNT

PROFILE PAGE
Access and manage your personal information:
- View/edit your name, email, and phone number
- Upload or change your profile picture
- Manage saved shipping addresses
- View your order history and track deliveries
- See your wishlist or saved items

ACCOUNT SETTINGS
Configure your preferences:
- Change your password
- Set notification preferences (email, push notifications)
- Manage privacy settings
- Add or remove payment methods

3.5 MESSAGES

Communicate directly with sellers:
- View all your conversations in the inbox
- Read and send messages in real-time
- Start new conversations from product pages
- Access message history anytime

3.6 SUPPORT

Need help? Visit the Support page:
- Browse Frequently Asked Questions
- Submit a support ticket
- Track your support requests
- Contact customer service

4. FOR SELLERS - BUSINESS GUIDE
================================================================================

4.1 DASHBOARD

Your seller dashboard is your business command center. Here you can see:
- Total sales and revenue summary
- Number of orders received
- Recent order activity
- Low stock alerts
- Sales performance charts
- Quick action buttons for common tasks

4.2 INVENTORY MANAGEMENT

ADDING PRODUCTS
1. Go to Inventory
2. Click "Add New Product"
3. Fill in product details:
   - Name and description
   - Price and stock quantity
   - Category and subcategory
   - Upload product images
   - Add specifications
4. Save the product to make it live

MANAGING EXISTING PRODUCTS
- View all products in a list or grid
- Edit any product by clicking on it
- Update stock quantities
- Change prices or descriptions
- Disable products temporarily
- Delete products permanently

4.3 ORDERS

PROCESSING ORDERS
- View all orders with status indicators
- Filter by status: Pending, Processing, Shipped, Delivered
- Click any order to see full details
- Update order status as you process them
- View customer shipping information
- Print packing slips if needed

4.4 PROMOTIONS

CREATING SALES
1. Go to Promotions
2. Click "Create New Promotion"
3. Choose discount type:
   - Percentage off (e.g., 20% off)
   - Fixed amount off (e.g., $50 off)
   - Buy X Get Y deals
4. Set start and end dates
5. Select which products are included
6. Save and activate

MANAGING PROMOTIONS
- View all active and past promotions
- Edit promotion details
- End promotions early if needed
- See promotion performance

4.5 ANALYTICS

Track your business performance:
- Sales overview (daily, weekly, monthly)
- Revenue trends and comparisons
- Best-selling products
- Average order value
- Customer demographics
- Traffic sources
- Download reports for your records

4.6 CUSTOMER ENGAGEMENT

REVIEWS AND RATINGS
- View all product reviews
- Respond to customer reviews
- See your average rating
- Track review trends

CUSTOMER QUESTIONS
- Answer product questions from buyers

- Track response times

4.7 MESSAGES

4.8 SETTINGS

SHIPPING SETTINGS

PAYMENT SETTINGS

STORE POLICIES

NOTIFICATIONS

6. HELP & SUPPORT
================================================================================

COMMON ISSUES

CANNOT LOG IN?
- Double-check your email and password
- Make sure Caps Lock is off
- Try resetting your password
- Clear your browser cache and cookies
- Try a different browser

PAGE NOT LOADING?
- Check your internet connection
- Refresh the page
- Try accessing from a different device

ORDERS NOT SHOWING?
- Check you're logged into the correct account
- Allow a few minutes for recent orders to appear
- Check your email for order confirmations

IMAGES NOT LOADING?
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
- Disable browser extensions temporarily

CONTACT SUPPORT

For technical issues or account problems:
1. Visit the Support page
2. Submit a support ticket with details
3. Include screenshots if helpful
4. Provide your account email
5. Describe the issue clearly

Response times typically range from a few hours to 24 hours depending on
the issue complexity.

TIPS FOR BEST EXPERIENCE
================================================================================

FOR BUYERS:
- Complete your profile for faster checkout
- Save frequently used addresses
- Read seller reviews before purchasing
- Check shipping times and policies
- Use the wishlist to save items for later

FOR SELLERS:
- Keep inventory updated to avoid overselling
- Respond to messages within 24 hours
- Process orders promptly
- Encourage customers to leave reviews
- Use high-quality product photos
- Write detailed, accurate descriptions
- Monitor your analytics to understand trends

FOR ADMINS:
- Check applications and tickets daily
- Maintain consistent moderation standards
- Keep platform policies up to date
- Monitor analytics for unusual activity

ACCOUNT SECURITY
================================================================================

PROTECTING YOUR ACCOUNT:
- Use a strong, unique password
- Don't share your login credentials
- Log out when using shared computers
- Enable two-factor authentication if available
- Keep your email address updated
- Report suspicious activity immediately
